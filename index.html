About Us

Education is the most powerful way to change destiny and get rid of poverty.

Our mission is to provide fair learning opportunities for poor children, help them get out of trouble through knowledge and reshape their future.

Through this platform, we are committed to connecting children in need with educational resources, volunteer tutoring, scholarship support and community help to break the learning barriers caused by poverty. We hope that every child can have hope, tools and direction to pursue their own dreams.

EduHope is not just a website, but also an action about educational equity and changing destiny. We believe that as long as everyone participates, every child deserves an unlimited future.

Let us work together to let education light up hope and let children see the future.
